THE PHILOSOPHY OF 1MOR

"One More Mile. One More Repeat. One More Second."

Project 1MOR isn't just a running club; it's a mental blueprint. Founded on the principle of progressive excellence, we inspire athletes of all skill levels to discover what lies beyond their perceived limitations. Whether you are stepping into running shoes for the first time or tapering for a sub-3 hour marathon, we run together, pacing each other to go 1 step further than yesterday.

Perseverance

Pushing through when your legs say stop but your mind says keep moving.

Community

No runner runs alone. Weekly group pacing, support groups, and camaraderie.

TRAINING PROGRAMS

Structured, goal-oriented programs drafted by certified running coaches.

Level: Novice

Couch to Community Run

Perfect for starting your running journey. This program alternates walking and jogging to build your base safely without injury risk.

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Weekly Commitment: 3 Days / Week
  • Average Volume: 5-10 Km / Week
  • Target: Running 20 minutes continuously

Sample Week Outline

Day 1: 5m Warmup Walk, 10x (1m Run, 1.5m Walk)
Day 2: Active Recovery / Mobility exercises
Day 3: 5m Walk, 8x (1.5m Run, 1m Walk)
Day 4: Rest Day
Day 5: 3km Easy Paced Walk/Jog combo
Day 6-7: Rest & Stretch
Level: Intermediate

Sub-25 Min 5K Blueprint

Engineered for runners looking to improve speed endurance, optimize stride frequency, and break personal records over the 5K distance.

  • Duration: 8 Weeks
  • Weekly Commitment: 4 Days / Week
  • Average Volume: 15-22 Km / Week
  • Target: Speed pacing & lactic threshold building

Sample Week Outline

Day 1: 4km Easy Recovery Run + 4 strides
Day 2: Intervals: 5x 800m @ target 5k pace (2m recovery walk)
Day 3: Rest / Strength Training
Day 4: 3km Tempo Run @ threshold effort
Day 5: Rest Day
Day 6: 6km Long Slow Run (aerobic base build)
Day 7: Full Rest
Level: Intermediate

10K Threshold Crusher

Focuses on raising your anaerobic threshold. Ideal for completing your first 10K or smashing through performance plateaus.

  • Duration: 8 Weeks
  • Weekly Commitment: 4 Days / Week
  • Average Volume: 25-35 Km / Week
  • Target: Fast, consistent endurance pacing

Sample Week Outline

Day 1: 5km Easy Zone 2 Aerobic Run
Day 2: Hills: 6x 45s hard uphill repeats (jog down recovery)
Day 3: Cross training / Core & glutes workout
Day 4: 5km Tempo Run: 1km warmup, 3km tempo, 1km cooldown
Day 5: Rest Day
Day 6: 8km Long Progression Run
Day 7: Recovery Walk & Rest
Level: Advanced

Half Marathon Pacing Formula

Designed to build critical stamina. You will master fueling strategies, active target pace pacing, and volume adaptations.

  • Duration: 12 Weeks
  • Weekly Commitment: 4-5 Days / Week
  • Average Volume: 35-50 Km / Week
  • Target: Half marathon completion or Sub-1:45 mark

Sample Week Outline

Day 1: 6km Aerobic Recovery Run
Day 2: Threshold: 3x 2km @ threshold pace (3m jog recovery)
Day 3: Easy Run 5km or Strength workout
Day 4: Rest Day
Day 5: 6km Paced Run (target Half Marathon Pace)
Day 6: 14km Long Run (steady effort)
Day 7: Full Rest / Deep stretching
Level: Elite

Peak Performance Marathon Plan

A comprehensive, high-volume block focusing on peak aerobic capacity, fuel storage capacity, and mental grit required to conquer the full 42.195 km.

  • Duration: 16 Weeks
  • Weekly Commitment: 5-6 Days / Week
  • Average Volume: 60-80 Km / Week
  • Target: Full marathon mastery and sub-4hr/sub-3hr goals

Sample Week Outline

Day 1: 8km Aerobic Easy recovery run + core
Day 2: Speed: 10x 400m hard (90s recovery jog)
Day 3: 10km Steady State Aerobic Run
Day 4: Rest Day
Day 5: 12km Tempo Run (holding marathon pace)
Day 6: 22km Long Endurance Run (with last 3km fast)
Day 7: Active Recovery Walk / Stretch
